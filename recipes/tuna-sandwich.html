The Tuna Sandwich

It's got fish. That's protein right?

Ingredients

  • Can of tuna you can afford
  • Mqyo
  • The cheapest bread that you can find
  • Mustard

Instructions

  1. Take 2 slices of bread and lay seperately on the counter.
  2. Drain the tuna and add to a bowl.
  3. Mix in mayo and mustard
  4. Spread the tuna mixture on the bread and assemble.
