Behold! The Poverty Spec Sandwich

Makes you remember the college days.

Ingredients

  • Generic Kraft Singles Cheese
  • Lunch Meat
  • The cheapest bread that you can find
  • Bonus points if you find a spare mayo packet

Instructions

  1. Take 2 slices of bread and lay seperately on the counter.
  2. Add the mayo to the bread now if you have it.
  3. Add meat and cheese on top of one slice of bread.
  4. Put the other slice of bread on top and enjoy!
