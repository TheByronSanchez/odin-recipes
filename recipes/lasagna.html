Making the World's Best Lasagna!

Super cheesy, super flavourful, simply Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Onions and Garlic
  • Sugar
  • San Marzano Tomatos

Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
  6. Enjoy with copious amounts of wine!
